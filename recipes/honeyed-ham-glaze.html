Honeyed Ham Glaze

Description

A sweet and spicy glaze for ham that's quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together honey, ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, minced onion, cloves, and ginger.
  2. Pour over ham slices as a glaze.
